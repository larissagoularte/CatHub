Calico

Calico cats, known for their tri-color coats and found in various breeds, are not a distinct breed themselves, but rather cherished for their unique quirks and individual personalities, with about one in every 3000 being male despite common belief.

Bengal

The Bengal cat combines the distinctive physical features of small forest-dwelling wildcats with the affectionate temperament of a family pet, resulting in unique characteristics not found in other domestic breeds.

Bombay

The Bombay is a medium-size cat with a well-balanced, muscular build, known for its friendly and outgoing nature, characterized by a medium-length body and tail, rounded head with wide-set ears, and large, rounded eyes.
